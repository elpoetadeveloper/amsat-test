AubieSat-1 was launched as part of NASA's Educational Launch of Nanosatellites (ELaNa) program. Its mission was to test two different types of solar panel encapsulation for degradation over time.

Following its October 2011 launch, the satellite transmitted the Auburn University battle cry, 'War Eagle' to Earth from space for the first time ever, in Morse Code. As of April 2013, the satellite continues to transmit!

The satellite has been sending battery voltage information down to Earth over the many months since launch, and Auburn University students are continually analyzing that information for clues as to what happened to the solar panels and the onboard batteries in the harsh environment of space.
