GRIFEX (GEO-CAPE ROIC In-Flight Performance Experiment)

Catalog number: 40379
Launch Date: Jan 31, 2015
Status: Operational

Downlink:
437.479MHz FM 9k6/19k2 GMSK AX.25

GRIFEX is a 3U CubeSat developed by MXL (Michigan eXploratory Laboratory) of the University of Michigan with the goal to perform engineering assessment of a JPL-developed all digital in-pixel high frame rate ROIC (Read-Out Integrated Circuit).

RF communications: UHF frequency band (437.485 MHz); output power: 1 W; modulation and encoding: GMSK, AX.25 protocol, 9.6 kbit/s; beacon: 10 s period, includes telemetry

More Info: https://directory.eoportal.org/web/eoportal/satellite-missions/g/grifex
