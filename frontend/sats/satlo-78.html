LituanicaSAT-1 is the first Lithuanian satellite mission. The primary mission objective is to provide university students and young engineers knowledge & real hands-on expertise in satellite engineering thereby helping to develop infrastructure and know-how in space technology by interdisciplinary interaction between academia and industry in Lithuania.

This mission is specially dedicated to honor the 80th Anniversary of the flight across the Atlantic by Lithuanian-American pilotts Steponas Darius and Stasys Girenas. Therefore the satellite bears the name of the original aircraft used during this historic flight - 'Lituanica'.
