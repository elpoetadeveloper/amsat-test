DEORBITSAIL

Catalog number: 40719
Launch Date: July 10, 2015
Status: Operational.

Packet Downlink:  145.975 MHz 1200bps BPSK AX.25
Beacon: 145.975 MHz 1200bps BPSK AX.25
Callsign: DOS

Mission: The DeorbitSail project is a collaboration to build a 3U CubeSat sized satellite with a deployable sail that will demonstrate rapid deorbiting.
Satellite Dimensions: 3U (10 cm x 10 cm x 34 cm)
Sail Dimension: 4 m x 4 m - Orbit: Polar Sun -synchronous
Altitude: 600 km. Deorbit Time: Less than 180 days
Launch Station: SLP, SDSC SHAR , India
Launch Date & Time: 10th July 2015 16:27 hrs UTC

Objectives:
Provide research in the field of deorbiting.
Provide a demonstrated and verified design for deorbiting of satellites and debris.
Provide effective and efficient in-space propulsion technologies based on solar.
