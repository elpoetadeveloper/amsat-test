UWE-2

Catalog number: 35934
Launched: September 23, 2009

Status: Operational

Downlink: 437.385 MHz FSK, 1k2 AFSK / 9k6 FSK AX.25 Transmit power of 0.5W

UWE-2 is the second cubesat built by the University of Wuerzburg in Germany.

It was launched via the PSLV (Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle) from Satish Dhawan Space Center in India.

The published downlink is 437.385 MHz (FSK, 1k2 AFSK / 9k6 FSK AX.25) with a transmit power of 0.5W.
