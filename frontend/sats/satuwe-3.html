UWE-3

Catalog number: 39446
Launched: November 21, 2013
Status: Operational

Downlink: 437.385 MHz, 9k6 baud FSK. Transmit power of 0.5W
Callsign: DP0UWG

The picosatellite UWE-3 is the third generation of a series of micro - satellites of the University of Wuerzburg.

In order to cover another important milestone in a development program for the realization of small satellite formations, is in the education of students, the primary technical objective to revise the existing attitude determination system and to extend a simple position control system.

Thus, the development of technical bases for the realization of robust pico - swarms to be brought forward. The secondary technical aim of the UWE-3 mission is to test the latest developments robust hardware and software designs on COTS - based in real space environment , so as to demonstrate the services here progress
