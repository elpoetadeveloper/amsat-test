CO-65 CUTE-1.7+APD II

Catalog number: 32785
Launched: June 30,2003
Status: Operational

Uplink: 1267.60 MHz, FM FSK 9k6 (AX.25)
Downlink: 437.475 MHz, FM FSK9k6/AFSK1k2 AX.25
Beacon: 437.275 MHz, FM CW

Cute-1.7 + APD II is the successor of the CUTE-I nano-satellite and Cute-1.7 + APD developed and built by the second generation of students of the Tokyo Institute of Technology Matunaga Laboratory for Space System (LSS).

CUTE 1.7 + APD 2 has an redesigned satellite structure for increased solar cell area. With a length of 2 Cubesat units and a height of 1.5 Cubesat units it is not compliant with the Cubesat standard.

The Avalanche Photo Diode sensor module, or APD, embarked on this 20cm x 15cm x 10cm nano-satellite was also developed by the Tokyo Institute of Technology Kawai Laboratory.

Following equipments are necesarry for this service.

1.Transceiver and TNC compatible with high-speed packet communication (GMSK 9600bps)

2. 1200 MHz band transmitting set, high-gain antenna

3.  430 MHz band receiving set

In addition the following equipment would be helpful.

4.satellite tracking equipments (for antenna rotation control and Doppler shift control)
